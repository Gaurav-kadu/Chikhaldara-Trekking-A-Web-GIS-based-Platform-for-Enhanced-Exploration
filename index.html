About

This Web GIS platform is developed to enhance the trekking experience in the Chikhaldhara region by providing an interactive map, detailed trail highlights, and essential information for adventurers. Created as part of a college project, the platform seamlessly integrates geospatial technology with real-world exploration. It aims to serve as a comprehensive guide for trekking enthusiasts, enabling easier navigation, better planning, and a safer, more informed outdoor experience.
